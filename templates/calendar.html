How It Works

Here's how the Pick-a-Date fundraiser works:

  1. Select one or more dates from the calendar. Each date you pick helps us raise money.
  2. The cost corresponds to the day you select. For example, day 12 costs $12.
  3. After selecting your dates, click "Cross Off" to move to the payment page.
  4. On the payment page, fill out your details and complete the process to finalize your donation.

This is a fun and interactive way to support us and see how much progress we make together!
